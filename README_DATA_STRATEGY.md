## Data Collection Process

### For Each Company:

**1. Primary Research (8 hours)**
- Read latest 10-K (SEC filing)
- Read annual report
- Review 2-3 HBS/Stanford cases
- Analyze earnings calls

**2. Secondary Research (4 hours)**
- Industry reports (IBISWorld, Statista)
- Market share data (IDC, Gartner)
- News analysis (WSJ, Bloomberg)
- Academic papers

**3. Structure & Validate (4 hours)**
- Fill framework templates
- Cross-reference data
- Cite all sources
- Quality check

**4. Faculty Review (2 hours)**
- MBA professor reviews
- Validates accuracy
- Approves for publication

**Total: 18 hours per company**
**Quality: MBA-level analysis**
